The Interface

Pre-Selector is minimal by design. Mantaining MacOS interface design, it layers the app in windows.

Importing Images

just drag and drop your camera images folder directly into Pre-Selector or clic the folder icon. Don't worry about having duplicates of RAW files and JPG files, Pre-Selector asosiate both files into one.

You can also go to File > Import Images or pressing ⇧ + ⌘ + I

Rating and Selecting Images

To select your images for compressing, click the upper-right box of the image you want to select. You can also select and rate from the Preview Window.

To rate them, use the stars located at the bottom of the image. You can then filter your images by number of stars.

You can select and deselect all images on the projects using this option on the toolbar menu.

Preview Mode

Priview mode let you view your image in full detail. You can also rate and select from this window.

To enter Preview Mode just double click the image or single click and hit the space bar on the keyboard.

Filtering Selection

Filtering comes handy when having a lot of images on one single project. After you rate your images you can filter them by the numbers of stars you gave them, "All" and "Not Rated".
