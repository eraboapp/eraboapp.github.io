Bulk Watermark

Pre-selector can bulk watermark or bulk stamp your own custom logo in all the images you select. Just go to Preferences > General

Uploading Custom Logo

To Upload your custom logo, click the "Select File" Buton on the "Load Logo" section.

To Cahnege the logo, just upload a new one. To remove the logo, click the x on the right side of the name of your image.

Opacity

Opacity will add transparency to your logo. You can control how transparent you want your image to be by manipulating the "Opacity bar" in the "Opacity" Section.

Angle

Angle will rotate your logo or image in any degree of freedom by using the "Angle Bar"

Size

Size will change the widht and height of your logo or image. You can control how big or small you want your logo to be by manipulating the "Size bar" in the "Size" Section.

Template

Template will change drastically the layout of your logo or image. This and all other options can be combined to get 100+ diferent logo positions to suit your particular style.
