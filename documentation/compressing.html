Compressing

With Erabo Photoselector you can pre-select and compress any photography session; or bulk resize your images and share them with your clients. Erabo also keeps the original files in a separate folder thus allowing you to edit them later once a client decides the photos they like.

Change Compression Size

You can choose the resulting image's size by going to Preferences > Behaviour. There you can change the resulting image's width. You don't need to worry about they height, Erabo resizes images preserving its aspect ratio.

Moving vs Coping

After compression is completed, Erabo Pre-Selector will ask if you want to move or copy the original images ( RAW Files or Original Files).

Moving may be a lot faster, though this action will move images out of the camera folder to the folder you select.

Copying will create a copy of the selected images on the destination folder. This method may take longer and will consume more storage. When in doubt, choose this option.

Folder Structure

Pre-Selector will generate a Project Folder with 2 Folders inside:

Originals: Contains the original images, no with resize or filters applied. Usually this folder contains your RAWs image files (the ones produced by your camera).

Preselection: Contains copies of the images you preselected, size and filters applied.
